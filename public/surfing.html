Surfing in Tamil Nadu

Ride the waves, embrace the thrill

Catch the Perfect Wave

Tamil Nadu is fast becoming a surfer's paradise. With its pristine coastline, moderate swells, and warm waters of the Bay of Bengal, it’s an ideal destination for both beginners and seasoned surfers.

Explore the golden beaches, feel the salt breeze in your hair, and learn the art of riding waves with professional surf schools spread across the coast.

Top Surfing Spots

Covelong (Kovalam)

Just south of Chennai, Covelong is the most popular surfing spot in Tamil Nadu. Known for consistent waves and a chill surf culture.

Mahabalipuram

This ancient town offers a mix of heritage and surf. Waves here are perfect for intermediate surfers looking for a mellow ride.

Manapad

A hidden gem on the southern coast, Manapad is ideal for adventurous surfers looking to avoid crowds and explore new breaks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is surfing safe for beginners?
Yes! Tamil Nadu's beaches have options for all skill levels. Instructors and lifeguards are present at popular surf spots.
Do I need to bring my own gear?
No. Most surfing schools and beaches offer rental equipment including boards and safety gear.
When is the best time to surf in Tamil Nadu?
The ideal surfing season is from June to September when the waves are consistent and the weather is favorable.
