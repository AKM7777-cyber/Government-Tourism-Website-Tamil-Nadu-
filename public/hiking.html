Hiking Trails of Tamil Nadu

Explore serene landscapes and misty mountains

Reconnect with Nature

Tamil Nadu offers a diverse range of hiking trails — from mist-covered hills to dense forest paths and heritage trails that tell stories of ancient civilizations. Whether you're a casual walker or a seasoned trekker, you'll find a trail here that soothes the soul and challenges your senses.

Experience the sunrise from a mountaintop, walk alongside cascading waterfalls, and trek through lush green valleys. Adventure and tranquility await you on every path.

Popular Hiking Trails

Kodaikanal Forest Trails

Walk through pine forests, meadows, and misty hills. Kodaikanal offers moderate hikes that are perfect for a peaceful day in the wild.

Western Ghats Ranges

Trekking through the UNESCO-listed Western Ghats offers rich biodiversity and panoramic views of valleys, rivers, and tea plantations.

Yelagiri Hills

A beginner-friendly destination with light trails, local villages, and a pleasant climate all year round. Great for weekend adventures.
