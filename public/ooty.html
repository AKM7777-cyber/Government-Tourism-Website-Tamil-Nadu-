Welcome to Ooty

The Queen of Hill Stations in Tamil Nadu

About Ooty

Nestled in the Nilgiri Hills, Ooty (Udhagamandalam) is a breathtaking hill station known for its lush greenery, colonial charm, sprawling tea gardens, and cool climate. It's a haven for nature lovers, honeymooners, and foodies alike.

Must-Visit Attractions

Botanical Gardens

Explore exotic plants and ancient trees in this iconic British-era garden.

Doddabetta Peak

The highest peak in the Nilgiris offering panoramic views of the Western Ghats.

Tea Factory & Museum

Learn how tea is made and enjoy fresh Ooty tea amidst rolling plantations.

Local Cuisine

Don’t miss out on piping hot varkey biscuits, Ooty chocolates, and local hill vegetables. The town also offers unique twists on South Indian dishes adapted to the colder climate.

Where to Stay

Ooty offers a range of accommodations, from cozy budget cottages to luxurious heritage hotels. Popular choices include the Fern Hill Palace, Sterling Ooty Elk Hill, and charming homestays that offer authentic Nilgiri hospitality.

Shopping in Ooty

Take home aromatic tea leaves, essential oils, homemade chocolates, woolen clothing, and Toda handicrafts from the bustling markets of Charing Cross and Commercial Road.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: March to June or September to November for the best weather.
  • Carry warm clothes, even in summer—it can get chilly in the evenings.
  • Local transportation includes taxis, autos, and rented bikes for convenience.
