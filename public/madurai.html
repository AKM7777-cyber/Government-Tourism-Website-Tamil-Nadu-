Madurai

The Ancient City of Temples & Culture

About Madurai

Known as the cultural capital of Tamil Nadu, Madurai is famous for its stunning Meenakshi Amman Temple, vibrant bazaars, and rich Tamil heritage dating back over 2,500 years.

Top Attractions

Thirumalai Nayakar Mahal

A 17th-century palace with Indo-Saracenic architecture and dramatic lighting shows.

Gandhi Memorial Museum

Explore Mahatma Gandhi’s life and preserved artifacts in this historic museum.

Culture & Festivals

The Chithirai Festival features divine wedding reenactments, traditional music, and Bharatanatyam performances.

Where to Stay

Stay at The Gateway Hotel, Heritage Madurai, or budget places near the temple area.

Shopping

Buy cotton sarees, brass lamps, jasmine garlands & temple souvenirs from Pudumandapam Market.

Travel Tips

October to March offers pleasant weather and festive celebrations.
The city is walkable with autos and cabs available for longer trips.
Respect temple rules, remove footwear, and dress modestly.

Tamil Taste Trails

Discover the flavors of Tamil Nadu – one dish at a time.
