About Chennai

Chennai, the capital city of Tamil Nadu, is a vibrant blend of traditional culture and modern growth. Known for its classical music, sandy beaches, ancient temples, and spicy cuisine, it’s a must-visit destination for travelers seeking heritage and warmth.

Top Attractions in Chennai

Marina Beach

The longest urban beach in India, famous for its sunrise views and evening walks.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

A magnificent Dravidian-style temple dedicated to Lord Shiva in Mylapore.

Government Museum

Home to rich collections of archaeology, art, and numismatics in Egmore.

Cultural Vibes

Chennai is renowned for the Margazhi Music Festival, Bharatanatyam dance performances, and traditional Carnatic concerts. Don’t miss the authentic filter coffee and the saree shopping experience in T. Nagar!

Local Delicacies

Relish spicy Chettinad curries, crispy dosas, and sweet Pongal in one of the many iconic eateries. Try Saravana Bhavan or Murugan Idli Shop for an authentic Chennai meal.
