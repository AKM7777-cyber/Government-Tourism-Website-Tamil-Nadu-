Kanyakumari

Where the Bay of Bengal, Arabian Sea & Indian Ocean Converge

Kanyakumari, located at the southernmost tip of India, is a place of spiritual significance, cultural fusion, and breathtaking natural beauty. It's the only place where you can witness the sunrise and sunset from the same beach.

Top Attractions

Vivekananda Rock Memorial

A sacred monument built in honor of Swami Vivekananda, located on a rocky island just off the coast.

Thiruvalluvar Statue

This towering statue of the Tamil poet Thiruvalluvar stands majestically beside the rock memorial.

Sunset View Point

One of the most mesmerizing sunset views in India—don’t miss the colors over the tri-sea confluence.

Culture & Experience

Kanyakumari is home to centuries-old temples, vibrant coastal festivals, and a harmonious mix of different cultures and faiths.

Local Delicacies

Enjoy the coastal flavors with dishes like fish curry, coconut-based gravies, banana chips, and southern-style filter coffee served with a view of the sea breeze.

Where to Stay

Kanyakumari offers a variety of accommodations from resorts to budget stays close to all key attractions.

Shopping

Local markets in Kanyakumari are full of handicrafts, seashell artifacts, jewelry, and traditional textiles—perfect for souvenirs.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: October to March.
  • Carry light cotton clothes, sunscreen, and sunglasses.
  • Try catching both sunrise and sunset from the beach.
  • Use local taxis, autos, or walk to nearby places.
