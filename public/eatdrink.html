Flavors of the South

Tamil Nadu cuisine is a celebration of rich spices, tangy tamarind, coconut, and rice. From roadside idli stalls to five-star Chettinad platters, the state offers a delicious spread for every foodie.

Must-Try Dishes

Idli & Sambar

A comforting breakfast combo made of steamed rice cakes and lentil stew.

Chettinad Chicken

A fiery specialty loaded with pepper, fennel, and South Indian spices.

Filter Coffee

Strong, frothy and served in traditional dabara set—unmissable in Tamil Nadu!

Where to Eat

  • Murugan Idli Shop – Authentic tiffin in Chennai & Madurai
  • The Bangala – A Chettinad cuisine gem in Karaikudi
  • Banana Leaf Meals – Served at most messes, especially in Coimbatore

Foodie Tips

  • Try meals served on banana leaves—they’re traditional and eco-friendly.
  • Don’t miss regional sweets like Mysore Pak and Jangiri.
  • Street food is safe in busy markets—just follow the locals.
